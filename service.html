Services


Financial Services from Partner Banks and NBFC

Unnati Banking Service is a transformative solution that ushers in a new era of transparency in farming-related transactions. It goes beyond merely fulfilling working capital requirements; it empowers farmers with the financial agility to seamlessly acquire all necessary production inputs. Furthermore, it equips them with the acumen to expertly manage sales receivables, allowing them to approach their agricultural endeavors with the precision and efficiency of a thriving business, thereby creating a profound impact on the farming landscape.


Agronomy Services

With technology powered insights on soil, weather, farming history, we support farm entrepreneurs through the farming lifecycle which ensures significant improvements in quality and quantity of produce.

Output Management Services

With a steadfast commitment to optimizing farmers' yields, we facilitate their enrollment on a dynamic platform that not only connects them to an extensive network of potential buyers but also extends essential financial support for their operational needs. This approach is meticulously designed to ensure that farmers secure the best possible returns for their agricultural produce while simultaneously addressing their working capital requirements.

Agri-Inputs Services

We provide unwavering guidance to farmers throughout every phase of their farming journey. Leveraging Artificial Intelligence, we equip them with valuable insights on the precise application of products, optimal timing for application, and the arrangement of a cost-effective agri input portfolio. Our goal is to empower farmers with the knowledge and resources necessary for making informed decisions that maximize productivity and efficiency in their farming practices.
