Our Partners


Creating a digital ecosystem in collaboration with agriculture-focused enterprises

At Unnati, our unwavering focus revolves around the creation of a robust, dependable, and sustainable ecosystem for farmers, meticulously cultivated through strategic collaborations with an array of dedicated partners. This collaborative approach is at the heart of our mission, working in tandem with diverse stakeholders to build a thriving agricultural community that stands the test of time and delivers lasting benefits to farmers and their communities



Agri-Input Brands

Unnati works with leading brands of crop nutrients, crop protection, seeds and implement manufacturers for expanding reach of their products to a wider target group. Additionally we also help them execute campaigns to educate and activate farmers and retailers.

We partner with various food processor companies and large traders enabling them to source products directly from the farmers.

We engage with them for specific varieties and with preferred specifications, through the complete farming lifecycle. This ensures traceability and adherence to quality specifications for the buyer. The farm entrepreneurs gain through sustainable business relationships.

We work with various foundations, FPOs and retail companies enabling them to expand their business by integrating all the value chain activities on the tech platform. Agri-retailers have been powered with a customised ustore app which enables them to improve their business efficiencies and cater to the farmers more effectively.
